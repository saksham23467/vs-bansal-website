## Quarterly reconciliation discipline

Most notices we see trace back to reconciliation gaps between books, GSTR-2B, and vendor credits. Treat **monthly** matching as the default above ₹50Cr turnover, and at least **bi‑monthly** for everyone else.

## E‑invoice readiness

If you are approaching threshold limits, pre‑stage masters early—HSN maps, party GSTIN validation, and ERP cut‑over tests—so first‑month noise does not miss filing windows.

## Working capital & ITC hygiene

Document vendor declarations, reverse‑charge calls, and import scenarios in a shared evidence pack. It reduces scramble when auditors or banks ask for traceability.

When in doubt, align with your CA **before** adopting an aggressive ITC position—a short memo today beats a lengthy inquiry later.
