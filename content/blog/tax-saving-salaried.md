## Know your regime election

Locking into the old vs new regime is a **multi‑year** choice for many earners. Model two scenarios with your Form 16, 26AS, and expected bonus cycles before optimising ELSS or NPS top‑ups.

## Allowances & reimbursements

Some components—components tied to actuals like LTA, telephone, or eligible reimbursements—still matter under policy design. HR and finance should sync before March proofs land.

## Capital gains & RSUs

If you exercise RSUs or sell ESPP positions mid‑year, coordinate with payroll on perquisite estimates so advance tax instalments stay honest.

> Planning works best when documentation is boring: predictable uploads, single source of truth for AIS mismatches, and early queries to issuers.
